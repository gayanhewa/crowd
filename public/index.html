So what is Tripsta ?

Tripsta , is a Mobile Q/A app. But , there are plenty of site for the same purpose right ? Well , Tripsta attempts to to standout. The initial rollout would be a Q/A app tagged by location. And , the Application would also , let you ask direct questions from its member's via SMS/Voice. Well there is a catch , the Community powered Q/A is free for all but to contact a user you will have to spend some coins. Will get to that in the next round.

FAQ

Who is behind Tripsta ?
Tripsta is backed by a bunch of enthusiastic web developer's from Asia.
What are the target platforms ?
Initial release will be targetted to Android users with a mobile site available for rest of the users to take advantage of this platform.
How much will it cost me to use Tripsta ?
Well Tripsta is a free service for everyone who uses the Q/A feature. If you want to go ahead and contact a user , you will have to pay a certaint fee. This fee will be divided amount the User and Tripsta. So it's a win-win situation.
Can enyone make money from Tripsta ?
Technically yes. When ever anothe User contacts you via SMS or Voice , you will be paid an agreed amount in cash.
Ok, this is cool. So how much will I earn per contact ?
Well , we haven't finalized the Pricing structure yet. So soon as we launch the 2nd phase all existing users will be notified.
